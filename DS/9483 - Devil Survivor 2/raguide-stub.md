# Introduction

- your best friend shares a new app with you that lets you see "death clips" of people
- after signing up, you and your friend get death clips! ...of each other. at the train station you're currently standing at with a train derailing.

- turn based grid game, you control teams of 3 (a human leader and two demons) and pit them against various enemies
- you recruit demons by bidding for them in auctions
- game follows a day structure with timed events that you pick out
- over the course of the game you learn about the nature of the disaster Japan is facing and eventually can choose how you wish to address the crisis

- game requires multiple playthroughs to complete: at least 3 full playthroughts if you are incredibly clever with saving. likely 4 or 5
- set is designed so that the first playthrough can be done without reading any of this! none of it at all! just do whatever and come back to read up if you get stuck or want to further optimize a playthrough
- all but one of the challenge achievements are designed to be accomplishable on a New Game playthrough

# General Tips

- save after every combat mission
- maintain multiple in-game saves if you're unsure of things
- unless otherwise noted, Suspend feature exists and is supported. Use it! 
    - some achievement concepts cannot be tracked unless I tell you to not use this feature, but for the most part the set's pretty friendly about it
- auctions: they "refresh" between combat missions and after save loads. If you don't like what you're seeing, reset
- there are four stats: MG, ST, VI, AG
    - MG generally affects spell strength
    - ST affects some physical moves and their strength
    - VI adds HP and makes you take less damage
    - AG makes you move earlier in a combat relative to other actors and affects some moves strength
- some Skills and abilities require certain stat cutoffs on things you might not normally prioritize, which only really start mattering around Wednesday. Notable skills:
    - Power Charge
    - Dual Shadow
    - Magic Yin
    - Magic Yang

### No Free Battle Challenges

- these challenges are here because the game does reward you with a title for doing this
- to not completely invalidate this on NG+, you're banned from pulling in your OP demons to cheese everything until Last Day *out of the gate*
    - You are allowed to carry over auction progress, you're forced to carry over Compendium progress and you can carry over skills
    - You can't use the skills anyway until you hit stat reqs, which means you need levels, which means you won't be able to just Megidolaon your way through this
    - You are allowed auctions and compendium because you can't afford the good stuff, but maybe you made a sick demon the first time around and don't want to go through the whole auction process for it again
    - You are allowed to unlock Mitamas because they're just stat sticks and you're on a budget anyway, but if you think you can make use of them go for it
    - You are allowed to unlock New Game Plus bosses, but good luck defeating them without high level demons and being on a budget

Design note: the game also offers a "none of your team leaders hit 0hp ever during the game" title but that seems extreme even to me. This seems like a fair middle ground for attainable without involving too many resets.

Pain points:
    - Day 1 is fine, but you may want to prioritize getting XP onto Hero
    - Day 2, you may have a rough time with the first Fumi mission
    - Day 3, you may have a rough time with the Phecda gauntlet
    - Day 4, Megrez is going to be a significant pain
    - Day 5, Alioth could be dangerous
    - Day 6 will be very rough
If you can get through Day 6, you should have the knowledge and skill to complete the rest of the game.
